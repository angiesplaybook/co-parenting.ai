What it does

Everything your family needs, nothing it doesn't

AI Copilot

Your personal family coordinator

Ask anything. "What does Mia need for school tomorrow?" "Remind her dad about the dentist appointment." "Draft a message to the nanny about next week's schedule." Your AI copilot knows your family's whole story — and remembers everything so you don't have to.

What does Maya need for her sleepover Friday?
Maya's sleepover at Emma's is Friday at 6pm. She'll need her sleeping bag, allergy meds, and retainer. Her dad is dropping her off — he confirmed yesterday.
Can you remind him about the meds?
Done! Heads-up sent and added to the handoff notes.
De-escalation

Rewrite before you send

Tense messages happen. Before you hit send, your AI copilot can rewrite your message into neutral, child-centered language — reducing conflict before it starts. It keeps the meaning, removes the heat.

Your draft
You never tell me anything in advance. I found out about the field trip from another parent. This is exactly why this doesn't work.
Suggested rewrite
I wasn't aware of the field trip until recently. Going forward, could we share school notices as soon as they come in? That would help me stay on top of things for Maya.
